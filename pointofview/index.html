Point of View

"Vaaternurk" ("The Point of View") deals symbolically with topic individual – group. Individually (solo) one’s point of view is presented straightforwardly and clearly, sometimes with excessive eagerness and conviction. In a group (ensemble) qualities like flexibility and working towards a common goal become more important. Since the individualism is mostly more dominant than mutual support in our society, keeping a common goal will not go so easily and the focus fades. The composition is meant for both live performance and the U:Player app. In both cases the element of space and random choices play an important role.
